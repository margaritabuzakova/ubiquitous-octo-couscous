Sad news for Phillip Schofield

Our thoughts are with you during this difficult time.

Popular TV presenter Phillip Schofield joined his on-screen partner Holly Willoughby to present today's episode of This Morning, but it nearly didn't happen. Phillip, who has previously opened up about having a severe allergy to bee and wasp stings, recalled the scary moment he stood on a bee in his home last night.

​

Speaking to Holly, Phillip revealed that after he stood on the bee in his kitchen, he felt like he was descending down a dark tunnel, leading him to think he was about to go into anaphylactic shock. Phillip said, "My wife was ready with the EpiPen and she was saying, 'Do I do it now? Do I do it now?"
